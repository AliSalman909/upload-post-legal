Upload-Post Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Upload-Post collects, uses, and stores information when users access the service.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and store:
  • Uploaded media files
  • Tenant-scoped post data and publishing history
  • Connected social account metadata
  • Encrypted access tokens and related token metadata
  • Basic technical logs needed for security, debugging, and system operation

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information only as needed to operate the service, including to:

  • Store uploaded media
  • Process publishing requests
  • Maintain connected social platform integrations
  • Secure and monitor the service
  • Debug errors and improve reliability

3. OAuth and Connected Platforms

When you connect a third-party social media account, Upload-Post may store tokens and related metadata. Sensitive tokens are stored in encrypted form where supported by the system design.

4. Data Sharing

We do not intentionally sell user data. We may send required information to connected third-party platforms only when necessary to perform authorized publishing actions requested by the user.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect stored information, including encrypted token storage and access controls. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

6. Data Retention

Data may be retained for as long as needed to operate the service, maintain records, troubleshoot issues, or comply with legal obligations.

7. User Responsibility

Users are responsible for the content they upload and publish, and for ensuring they have authority to connect and use third-party accounts.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the service after updates means you accept the revised policy.
